How to Use CAD Visualizer

Welcome to CAD Visualizer! Follow these simple steps to transform your CAD models into stunning photorealistic images:

  1. Upload your CAD model: Click the "Upload" button and select your CAD file (e.g., .stl, .obj, .dwg, .dxf, .iges, .iges, .step, .stp, .3mf, .glb, .gltf, .ply, .off, .mtl, .blend).
  2. Customize your image: Choose the environment, lighting, and angle for your model.
  3. Generate the image: Click "Generate Image" to create a photorealistic render.
  4. View and download: Once generated, you can view the image in full resolution or download it.
  5. Sample CAD Files for Testing: Explore a collection of sample CAD files for testing purposes at Pimp My Drawing - Cars.

Enjoy creating beautiful visuals with CAD Visualizer!
